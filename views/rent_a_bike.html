Rent A Bike

Bikes are the most popular modes of transport in Goa. We rent a variety of bikes. This service is available 24/7, 365 days a year. Bike rentals are readily available here. Bardez, Pernem, are all major hubs. Along with the bikes we even offer helmets and some amount of fuel. Renting a bike is helpful because it can take you places where cars cannot reach and saves a lot of time by escaping the traffic quite efficiently.

Terms and Conditions
1.The user shall be not less than 18 years of age.
2.The user shall have a valid two wheeler /motorcycle driver's licence and an international driver's licence.
3.The user shall sign provide all necessary documents and deposits as prescribed.
4.A deposit must be made of a certain amount for reserving the bike. The balance must be paid when you pick the bike.
5. Renters are responsible for any damage to the bike. Renters are liable for the full replacement cost should the bike be lost or stolen.
6. In the unfortunate event of stolen bke, it is your responsibility to report this to the police. Please provide a copy of the declaration given at the police station to us.
6. Valid driver’s license, credit card and signed waiver required with all the rentals.
7. Bike type are subject to availability. Please call for availability and group rates.
