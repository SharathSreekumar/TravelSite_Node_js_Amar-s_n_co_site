Lakes & Waterfall

As soon as you hear Goa, you are ought to think about the long beautiful beaches and water flowing up to your feet. It is one of India’s best tourist destinations and for the people who only rely on the beaches come here and find out that, beaches are not the only places of attraction. Goa is full or lakes, springs and waterfalls as well. These are the places of relaxations coming away from the busy crowds on the roads. Goa, known to all as the land of golden beaches, is also gifted by mother nature with endless beauty. It's not just the beaches but Goa is also rich with a number of lakes, waterfalls and springs that make it a tourist's paradise. These waterfalls, lakes and springs away from the shores of sea, away from the hustle bustle of the beaches and thundering waves give you that perfect retreat, a perfect picnic spot and refreshing water to make a splash.
